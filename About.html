How It Works

1. Register

Housewives can sign up to offer their homemade meals, and students can sign up to place food orders.

2. Browse & Order

Students can browse the menu, explore different food options, and place orders directly through the website.

3. Enjoy Homemade Meals

Once an order is placed, housewives prepare the food, and students receive their delicious meals at their doorstep.

About Us

Welcome to HomeFeast - where homemade food meets students in need of a hearty meal. Our platform connects talented housewives with students living away from home, allowing them to enjoy delicious, home-cooked meals.

Our Mission

We believe that food is more than just sustenance; it's a way to connect people, cultures, and hearts. Our mission is to provide students with access to healthy, affordable, and delicious homemade meals, while also empowering housewives to share their cooking skills and earn a livelihood.
